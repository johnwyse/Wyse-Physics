Force

Force = (mass)(acceleration)
F = ma

source: https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/newtons-second-law-of-motion/

Variable Definition Symbol Unit
Force A push or pull on an object F Newton (N)
Mass The amount of matter in an object m kilograms (kg)
Acceleration The rate at which velocity changes a meters per second per second (m/s/s)
